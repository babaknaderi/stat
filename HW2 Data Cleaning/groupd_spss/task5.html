  2. In the “Target Variable” field write “VQ” and in the “Numeric Expression” field write the expression for converting a 7-point scale to a 5-point scale according to Köster et. Al research paper.

    3. (-0.0262*(VQ-1)**3 + 0.2368*(VQ-1)**2 + 0.1907*(VQ-1)+ 1)

    The formula assumes that the 7-point scale spans between 0 and 6. We adapt the formula for a 7-point scale from 1 to 7
