Example

Are the raters consistent over the time? Check the test-retest reliability of the test stimulus "maus_m_700_normAsl_-26" for all four repitions (i.e repition 1 vs repition 2, 2 vs 3 and 3 vs 4)

Setup and transfering data format

First, we need to check “no” in “Does your text file match a predefined format?”

Variables are delimited by specific character (i.e, comma) and variables are starting from line 1.

Check each line represents 1 case and we import all the cases.

Check comma ass delimiter.

Performing the test

Analyze > Correlate > bivariate > Add the rating 1 and rating 2 “Variables”

Analyze > Correlate > bivariate > Add the rating 2 and rating 3 “Variables”

Analyze > Correlate > bivariate > Add the rating 3 and rating 4 “Variables”

Output

correlation between rating 1 vs 2, 2 vs 3 and 3 vs 4
