Example

Use multiple linear regression to predict video quality (VQ) based on fragmentation (VF), unclearness (VU) and discontinuity (VD).

Setup and transfering data format

Choose Analyze → Regression → Linear…

Add VQ to “Dependent” and VF, VU and VD to “Independent(s)” → Click “Plots…” to compute residuals plot

Add *ZRESID to y-variable and *ZPRED to x-variable

Output

Output 1

Output 2

Result interpretation

A multiple linear regression was calculated to predict video quality (VQ) based on fragmentation (VF), unclearness (VU) and discontinuity (VD). A significant regression equation was found (F(2, 3375) = 3492.7, p < .000), with an R2 of .756.
Video quality is equal to 0.003 + 0.388 VF + 0.465 VU + 0.085 VD.
